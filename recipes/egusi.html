Egusi Soup

Description

Popular Nigerian Eba and Egusi Soup

This is the original Igbotic Egusi Soup served to good in-laws when they come to marry a special daughter.

Ingredients

  • 1Â½ cups ground egusi
  • 2 tablespoons dry ground achi
  • Bitter leaf
  • Beef
  • Shaki
  • Stockfish
  • 2- 3 cooking spoons red palm oil
  • 1 teaspoon Ogiri Igbo
  • 2 seasoning cubes
  • 2 tablespoons ground crayfish
  • Habanero / Scotch bonnet pepper
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Boil the meats and stockfish with the seasoning cubes till almost done.
  2. Add pepper, crayfish, ogiri Igbo, ground egusi, bitter leaves and palm oil
  3. Cover and cook for about 7 minutes
  4. Mix the ground achi with water till a medium consistency is achieved
  5. Cover and continue cooking till the soup thickens and has an overall yellow colour. This shouold take 5 to 7 minutes.
  6. Add salt to your taste and stir very well. Its done!
